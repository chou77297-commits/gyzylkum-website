Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026 | Version: 1.0

At GYZYL KUM LLC, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with our payment facilitation services. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued, the process for requesting a refund, and important information regarding chargebacks and disputed transactions.

1. Overview

GYZYL KUM LLC acts as a Payment Facilitator (PayFac), processing payments on behalf of merchants. As such, refunds are generally handled between you (the merchant) and your customers. However, there are specific circumstances where we may initiate refunds or where our refund policies apply directly to fees charged for our services.

Important Note:

Funds processed through our platform are held in settlement accounts and disbursed according to your payment schedule. Once funds have been settled to your bank account, we cannot reverse transactions—refunds must be issued by you directly to your customer.

2. Refunds for Service Fees

2.1 Monthly Fees

Monthly service fees (including account maintenance fees, minimum processing fees, and subscription fees) are generally non-refundable. If you believe you were charged in error, you may request a review within 30 days of the charge date.

2.2 Transaction Fees

Transaction fees are earned at the time of processing and are non-refundable, even if the underlying transaction is later refunded to the customer. This is because the processing services have already been rendered.

2.3 Chargeback Fees

Chargeback fees are assessed when a customer disputes a transaction. These fees are non-refundable, as they cover the administrative costs of handling the dispute process. If a chargeback is resolved in your favor, the chargeback fee may be refunded at our discretion, but the original transaction fee remains non-refundable.

2.4 Setup Fees

One-time setup fees are non-refundable once account setup and underwriting have been completed.

3. Refund Processing Timeline

When a refund is approved, the timeline for processing depends on the original payment method:

Payment Method Processing Time Notes
Credit/Debit Cards 3-5 business days Funds return to the cardholder's account; time varies by card issuer.
ACH/Bank Transfers 5-7 business days Bank processing times may vary.
Wire Transfers 1-3 business days Subject to intermediary bank processing.

4. Chargebacks and Disputes

4.1 What is a Chargeback?

A chargeback occurs when a customer disputes a transaction with their card-issuing bank. When this happens, the transaction amount is debited from your settlement account, and a chargeback fee is assessed.

4.2 Chargeback Process

  • Notification: You will receive notification of the chargeback via email and your merchant dashboard.
  • Response Window: You typically have 7-14 days to respond with evidence to dispute the chargeback.
  • Evidence Submission: You must provide documentation proving the transaction was valid (e.g., proof of delivery, signed contracts, communication records).
  • Resolution: The card issuer makes a final decision, which may take 30-90 days.

Preventing Chargebacks:

To minimize chargebacks, we recommend:

  • Clear communication with customers about products/services
  • Using clear billing descriptors that customers will recognize
  • Responding promptly to customer inquiries
  • Maintaining detailed records of all transactions

5. Refund Requests from Merchants

If you need to refund a customer for a transaction processed through our platform, you may initiate a refund through your merchant dashboard. Please note:

  • Refunds can only be processed for transactions that occurred within the last 180 days.
  • Refunds must be initiated from the same merchant account that processed the original transaction.
  • Partial refunds are supported and may be processed through your dashboard.
  • Once a refund is initiated, it cannot be cancelled.

6. Force Majeure

GYZYL KUM LLC shall not be liable for any failure or delay in refund processing caused by circumstances beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to acts of God, natural disasters, war, terrorism, riots, embargoes, acts of civil or military authorities, fire, floods, accidents, network infrastructure failures, strikes, or shortages of transportation facilities, fuel, energy, labor or materials.

7. Erroneous Refunds

If you receive a refund to which you are not entitled, you must immediately notify us and return the funds to GYZYL KUM LLC. Failure to do so may result in legal action to recover the funds and any associated costs.

8. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund of service fees or to dispute a charge, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your merchant dashboard and review the transaction or fee in question.
  2. Submit a refund request through your dashboard or contact our support team at support@gyzylkum.com.
  3. Include the transaction ID, date of transaction, amount, and detailed reason for your refund request.
  4. Allow 3-5 business days for us to review your request.
  5. If approved, refunds will be processed to your original payment method.

9. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a chargeback decision or fee assessment, you may file an appeal by contacting disputes@gyzylkum.com within 30 days. All appeals must include supporting documentation. Final decisions regarding disputes and chargebacks are made by the card-issuing banks and card network rules, and GYZYL KUM LLC's role is limited to facilitating the dispute process.

10. Updates to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or card network rules. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, through your merchant dashboard or email.
