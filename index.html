About GYZYL KUM

Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Delaware, USA, GYZYL KUM LLC is dedicated to providing high-quality technology consulting, cross-border trade support, and cybersecurity assessment services to businesses worldwide.

Our name, "GYZYL KUM," originates from the Kazakh language, meaning "precious sand." It symbolizes our commitment to cherishing every partnership and growing together with our clients through resilience and meticulous service.

Our team consists of experts from technology, finance, and legal sectors, bringing rich international experience and deep industry insights to help you succeed in the digital age.
