About Us

Founded in 2024 and headquartered in Delaware, USA, GYZYL KUM LLC is dedicated to providing high-quality technology consulting, cross-border trade support, and cybersecurity assessment services to businesses worldwide.

Our Story

The name "GYZYL KUM" comes from the Kazakh language, meaning "precious sand." It represents our philosophy: every partnership is precious, and through meticulous service and resilience, we grow together with our clients.

Our Team

We bring together experts from technology, finance, and legal sectors, combining international experience with deep industry knowledge to deliver exceptional results.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We build trust through honesty and transparency.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and technologies to solve complex challenges.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do.
